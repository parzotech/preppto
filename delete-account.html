Delete your Preppto account

This page explains how users of Preppto can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

About this app

App name: Preppto
Developer: Parzo Tech
Sign-in method: Google Sign-In (OAuth)

Preppto is an exam preparation app. If you signed in with Google, your account and related activity may be stored so the app can save your progress, test history, subscriptions, and other features.

How to request account deletion

To delete your Preppto account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to:
    parzotech.customer.support@gmail.com
  2. Use this subject line: Preppto account deletion request
  3. Send the email from the same Google account you used to sign in to Preppto.
  4. In your message, include:
    • Your full name
    • The email address linked to your Google account
    • A clear statement that you want your Preppto account and associated data deleted
Request account deletion by email

What data will be deleted

After we verify your request, we will delete account-linked data such as:

  • Account profile information (name, email, user ID)
  • Mock test history, attempts, scores, and review data
  • Practice test activity and progress
  • Daily challenge history
  • Exam planner progress, quiz results, and related activity
  • Performance analytics linked to your account
  • Consent and account-related app records stored for your user profile
  • Purchase and subscription records linked to your account in our systems, where deletion is permitted

What data may be kept

Some information may be retained where required by law, for security, fraud prevention, accounting, or dispute resolution. This may include:

  • Payment or billing records required by applicable law
  • Records needed to resolve disputes, enforce our terms, or comply with legal obligations
  • Minimal backup copies for a limited period, if applicable, before permanent deletion

Any retained data will be kept only for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was retained.

Processing time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days. We may contact you if we need additional information to verify your identity before completing the deletion.

Important notes

  • Deleting your Preppto account is permanent. You will lose access to saved progress, test history, planner progress, and purchased entitlements linked to that account.
  • If you have active subscriptions through Google Play, you may also need to cancel them separately in your Google Play account settings.
  • Signing out of the app does not delete your account or stored data.

Need help?

If you have questions about account deletion or your personal data, contact us at parzotech.customer.support@gmail.com.
